Merge Numbers

Drop numbers into columns. Match 3 or more to merge them into bigger numbers!

How to Play

  • Tap a column to drop a number
  • 3+ adjacent same numbers merge
  • Merged tiles become the next number
  • Chain reactions score bonus points
  • Don't let columns fill up!
